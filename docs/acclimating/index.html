Acclimating

For me, the last couple of weeks have been focused around work and trying to understand the nuances of the project at hand. Understandably, making improvements to the national health information system is an enormous task. There are a dizzying number of pressing issues to address and that makes prioritization a challenge. Next week I will travel to an outlying health facility to observe how clinical staff are trained to use the current version of the electronic health record software. That will definitely be interesting to see!

Tomorrow there is a Trade Fair in a nearby town. Hopefully that will provide lots of opportunities for photos. Then on Monday we will have a day off from work for a national holiday. We are planning to attend the festivities with some of our fellow Peace Corps volunteers. As much as I am eager to dig into the work, I am happy to have the opportunity to relax a bit and see more of the country.
