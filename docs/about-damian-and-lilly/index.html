About Damian & Lilly

Damian and Lilly finished their degrees at Oregon State University, got married, and are heading to Japan; it’s been a busy year!

Lilly was accepted into the Japanese Exchange and Teaching (JET) program and placed in Tome, Japan. She’ll be primarily teaching elementary schoolers at Minamikata Elementary School, though she’ll make weekly and monthly trips to 2 other elementary schools and 2 kindergartens for a total of 5 schools. Lilly is the first full time JET Assistant Language Teacher (ALT) that Minamikata Elementary has had.

Damian is continuing to work for Citycounty Insurance Services (CIS). The time difference means that he has to wake up really early to have 4 hours of overlap with Oregon. Since CIS is for Oregon cities and counties, they were a little unsure of having a remote worker from Japan but it’s been working out so far! Damian also didn’t have any Japanese skills upon first arriving. He has to spend a lot of time learning the various written languages used here as well as trying to learn the culture.

We’ll be in Tome for a full year, returning around mid-late August 2020.

Feel free to come visit us if you want!
