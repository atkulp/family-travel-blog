Coronavirus

Hello everyone!

I’m writing this brief post to give everyone an update on the situation here in Japan. Some of you may have heard that Abe Shinzo, Japan’s prime minister, made an announcement last week that all of the schools in Japan would be closed for the next several weeks. It was really a suggestion, but as my school district decided to close school, you may be wondering what all this means for Damian and I.

For the most part, our day-to-day doesn’t really change. Damian isn’t affected at all, as he works from home, and I’m actually still going to work. School is closed for the kids, but teachers are actually still required to go in.

So, for the next month until the new school year, I’m going to be spending a lot of time at my desk. I will probably be doing prep, Japanese language study, and discreet reading. In all honesty, I’m probably going to be extremely bored for the next month until the new school year starts (The school year starts in April in Japan). However, I will still be working and it’s pretty much business as usual.

Damian and I are doing fine, and we’re mildly amused with the antics of some people overreacting to the situation.

Look forward to some older posts that we’ve been working on! We’ll be sure to keep everyone updated with the goings on out here.
