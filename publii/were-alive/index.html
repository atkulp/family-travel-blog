We’re alive

Just in case anyone was concerned that we haven’t posted for a little bit, we just wanted to assure everyone that we are alive! We haven’t made it to any festivals recently and we’re still trying to figure out some of the basics that didn’t come with our apartment (we finally got a real stove, still working on an oven, got internet sorted, etc.)

We do have a few larger posts in the works, but it’s taking some time to collect some good pictures and to make sure we have enough experience to actually write a good post.

As to our person stuff: we’re doing alright! My work is going well and I’ve not had any issues remoting to Oregon (other than that I’m not really a morning person and 5am is definitely the morning!). Lilly is getting into the groove of normal teaching stuff and today is visiting her first kindergarten. We’re both excited about the addition of a stove since it means that we can finally cook more than one thing at once!

We’re also planning some visits to other parts of Japan, so keep an eye on the blog in the next few weeks. Lilly has some 3 day weekends that we’re going to make good use of, possibly going to a ryokan (旅館) or traditional inn with tatami matted rooms and public baths in the Sendai area.

So we’ve got some plans for the next few weeks, we’re finally getting our apartment setup more, and we’re definitely still alive, we just haven’t done anything terribly interesting for a week or two (sorry).
