さようなら！Sayonara!

Hello everyone, and goodbye for now! Today Damian and I get on a plane to Narita airport in Tokyo, where I’ll be oriented for three days before we are sent on our way to Tome on the shinkansen (新幹線), or bullet train! We’ll be leaving PDX at 11:45 AM Oregon time, and arriving in Japan at 2:00 pm their time on Sunday! So we’re losing Saturday afternoon and Sunday morning, nearly a whole day, in crossing the international dateline.

For those of you who are curious, Japan is 16 hours ahead of the Northwest, which means we’ll frequently be in tomorrow when you’re in today. WooOoOOoo, time travel!

We’re very excited, and we will probably be very busy, but we’ll be sure to keep everyone updated to the best of our ability.
