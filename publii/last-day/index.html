Last Day

Today is our last full day in Oregon! Lilly has a training in Portland followed by a lunch, we’ll overnight at a friend’s house, and then leave tomorrow morning at 11:45. We said goodbye to my parents on Wednesday and now it’s our turn!

Thank you to everyone that helped us clear our apartment! Lilly and I have decided that 3rd floor apartments are not so much fun to move out of. Things were rushed, but we love you all and couldn’t have done it without your help!

Finally, for those that were aware: Lilly’s surgery on Wednesday went ok and she’s been healing up well. She discovered she had a retinal tear in her left eye on Tuesday, they operated on Wednesday to spotweld the retina in place, and then she got to spend Thursday feeling like she’d been punched really hard in the face. Not the best timing in terms of us leaving the country, but at least her retina won’t detach in Japan!

We’re excited to be starting this journey and look forward to sharing lots of pictures and stories with everyone!
